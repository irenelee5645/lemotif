About ▾

Lemotif is a project by Alice Li & Devi Parikh in the Visual Intelligence Lab at Georgia Tech. Our goal is to develop a fun, intuitive journalling tool helping users understand patterns in their emotional states and associated sources over time, thereby supporting emotional well-being, self-reflection, and opportunities for action.
